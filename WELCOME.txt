================================
   WELCOME TO CODENVY
================================

Compilation of this application take a while, so be patient.

The app URL will show up a bit earlier than the app is actually deployed to the runner.

You will see a message that app output has been disabled.

Normally, build takes 2-3 mins, but the app URL shows up a bit earlier.

Check app URL in 2 mins after the URL shows up.

You may get logs at Run > View Logs to see the progress of the run process.
